Hi! I am Georgie Zurriaga Nacion!

But people know me more as Onika. I am a fresh graduate from Mindanao State University - General Santos City. I studied Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Networks.

I have this immovable love for art and music!

I wanted to study and pursue my passion but because of financial issues, I settled with the scholarship that I got from MSU-GSC and enrolled in the course with the shortest line. Before I entered the university, I was downcasted and bitter. Little did I know that the closed doors before me was to open a new dimension and form of art for me to explore.

Front-End Development, UI/UX Designing, Fullstack Development

Because of how I got into Information Technology, I was having a hard time studying (no, I did not have bad grades). Learning how to code was hard for me because of my grief. I had CS/IT senpais and my sensei to help me during my first year in college. They witnessed all my struggles and how I was about to give up without even trying to survive the course. I never appreciated my course not until I had my internship in the ICTO department of MSU-GSC. I had my first encounter with Figma and I was designing the dashboard for the proposed Student Portal for MSU-GSC. A staff told me that I could be a great developer and that I should pursue it. His simple words encouraged me and after my duty, I found myself studying and learning more about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Long story short, I fell in love with it (and I still have a long way to go)!

My Introverted Lifestyle

Right after graduation, I had job offers from different companies from my city and even outside the country. However, upon observing my performance during my internship and even throughout my college years, I find myself more productive working at night... in my room. The job offers were great but what good can a great offer do compared to a working set up that naturally unleashes your productivity? (Yes, I am a huge introvert but I still have a life outside my room. I am a normal 22 year old kid who doesn't talk much but still gets adopted by extroverted adult friends).
